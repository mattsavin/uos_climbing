The Arena.

We are a group of dedicated climbers representing The University of Sheffield across the UK.

BUCS

British Universities & Colleges Sport

The pinnacle of university sport. We send our top athletes to compete against the best climbers from universities across the entire UK. It's highly competitive and our primary focus for the elite squad.

NUBS

Northern Universities Bouldering Series

A fantastic bouldering competition series spanning multiple rounds across Northern universities. Perfect for everyone from beginners to Team GB climbers looking for regular competition structure and a great atmosphere.

NULSCC

National Universities Lead & Speed Climbing Competition

For the rope specialists! NULSCC focuses on lead climbing and speed climbing. If you prefer the endurance and technicality of roped ascents, this is our main lead competition focus.

MUCC & Varsity

Midlands Series & Sheffield Derby

We also participate in the Midlands University Climbing Competition (MUCC), and of course, the highly anticipated Sheffield Varsity, where we face off against Sheffield Hallam University. Pride on the line!
