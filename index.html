Welcome to USMC Indoor & Competitions, Sheffield's indoor climbing and competition branch within University of Sheffield Mountaineering & Climbing Club (USMC). Social sessions, structured training, and a full competition programme from your first visit to the wall to BUCS finals.

Open to Everyone

Never touched a wall before? Perfect. Our weekly social sessions are relaxed, friendly, and free from pressure — just turn up and give it a go.

Regular coached sessions to build technique, strength, and movement — whether you're working on your first V3 or chasing V8.

Competition Pathway

Compete at the highest university level — NUBS, BUCS, NULSCC, Varsity, and more. A clear route from your first comp to the national finals.

Whether you're looking to push your limits in BUCS or just looking for a friendly group to boulder with on Tuesdays, there's a place for you.
