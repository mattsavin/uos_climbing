The Splinter

The University of Sheffield Climbing Club (USCC) is a brand new student sport club, officially launching next month. We were born out of a desire for greater autonomy and a specific vision for what a university climbing team should look like.

Previously operating under the umbrella of the USMC (University of Sheffield Mountaineering Club), we realized that our core interests—primarily indoor climbing and high-level bouldering/lead competitions—required a dedicated structure. The USMC's broad scope didn't provide us with the focused autonomy we needed to support our growing competitive squad and our regular indoor climbing community.

By declaring independence, we are now able to build a community perfectly tailored to modern gym climbers and competitive athletes, while remaining incredibly welcoming to completely new climbers looking to learn the ropes.
